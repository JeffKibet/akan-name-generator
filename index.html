About

This web application calculates the day of the week a user was born and assigns them a corresponding Akan name based on their gender. Inspired by the naming traditions of Ghana, where names reflect the day of birth, the app combines cultural insight with basic programming logic to deliver a personalized result.








How to Use the Akan Name Generator

To use the Akan Name Generator, simply follow these steps:

1. Enter your date of birth in the provided date input field.
2. Select your gender by choosing either "Male" or "Female".
3. Click the "Send" button to submit your information.
